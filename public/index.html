About

Bridging Clinical Medicine, Trauma-Informed Performance, and Systems Strategy

Dr. Rajaipour is a board-certified family medicine physician, U.S. Navy officer, entrepreneur, and author who bridges clinical medicine, trauma-informed performance, and systems strategy.

Board-Certified Family Medicine Physician · U.S. Navy Officer · Founder, VitaRegen Medical · Co-author, The Resurrection Algorithm

Her work integrates clinical insight with operational strategy, serving leaders navigating high-stakes transitions, performance challenges, and organizational reinvention. She developed The E3 Method™ — a three-phase clinical resilience system for sustained leadership coherence.

Dr. Rajaipour holds an MD from Windsor University School of Medicine and completed her family medicine residency at Doctors Hospital in Columbus, Ohio. She is co-author of the Amazon #1 International Bestseller The Resurrection Algorithm alongside Brian Tracy and Medhat Zaki, and has been featured on the Voices in Health & Wellness podcast, SDVoyager, and CanvasRebel.
